SALES ADMIN
ASSISTANT

A sales administrative assistant provides clerical and minor technical support for the sales department of a company or major organization. Recordkeeping responsibilities may include sales and expense reports, electronic databases and sales proposals for prospective customers. Administrative assistants also plan meetings and ensure that sales representatives in the field are in contact with home office staff. Report directly to the management of the sales department, though they work independently with minimal oversight. Sales administrative assistants may advance through promotion to other positions with increased responsibility within the company.
